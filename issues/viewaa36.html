Summary0000914: Unexpected error while updating a contact duplicate
DescriptionOn v2.2-beta1, if you try to create a contact whose name is exactly or almost like the one of an existing contact, the "Possible duplicates found" popup will open (GREAT!!! :) ).
But if you select one of the duplicates found, and click on the "Update contact" button, you will get an Unexpected error and the contact duplicate will not be updated, but a new contact will actually be created !
