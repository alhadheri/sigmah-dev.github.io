Summary0000982: No saving of content of contact list field with individual/organisation limitation
DescriptionIn v2.2-rc1, in a contact model, the content of a contact list field limited to accept only organisations is not stored.

This issue is also reproduced on a contact list field limited to 1 individual, and used in a project model.
Steps To Reproduce1. in a contact model, create a contact list field configured to accept only organisations
2. create a contact from this model
3. add an organisation in this list of contacts
4. click on Save
5. open the "History" tab => you see that your modification has been saved in the history
6. come back on the "Details" sub-tab
=> your contact is no more present in the list of contacts, as it is not present in the "Relations" tab
