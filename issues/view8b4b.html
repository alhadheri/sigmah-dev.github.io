Summary0000492: Logframe content cleared when copy-pasted elsewhere without prior saving
DescriptionThe content of a logical framework which has not been saved before being copied and pasted elsewhere will be cleared without any notification to the user.
Steps To Reproduce1. Fill the content of a logframe (without clicking on the save button)
2. Click on the "Copy" button
3. Open another project
4. Open the "Logical Framework" sub-tab
5. Click on the "Paste" button
6. Go back to the screen where you have copied this logframe
The logframe is now empty !
