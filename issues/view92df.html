Summary0001108: Impossible to edit Contact field in closed phase even with relevant privilege
DescriptionOn v2.2-rc6, if a user has the "Modify content of closed phases/projects" global privilege, he won't be able nevertheless to edit a contact field in a closed phase.         Field 'NAME_OF_CONTACT_FIELD' with latest value '' could not be emptied because it is locked.
