Summary0000364: Add user/server/date to bug report email title
DescriptionAll bug reports send via the "Report a bug" have a titled like "[fr] Rapport de bogue".
As a consequence, when they are processed to be transformed from an email sent to support@sigmah.org to a forum post, they are not created as new forum topic, but as comment to the first forum topic holding the title "[fr] Rapport de bogue".

To prevent this, some other informations should be added to the auto-generated title of the mail.
Instead of "[fr] Rapport de bogue", the title should be "[fr] Rapport de bogue (#USER_EMAIL#/#HOST_URL#/#DATE#)".
For example: "[fr] Rapport de bogue (demo@sigmah.org/demo.sigmah.org/31-08-2011)"
