Summary0000179: Add "Sites count" indicator geographical aggregation method
DescriptionSome users have requested to have a new aggregation method: "Sites count". This aggregation method is used only on the geographical dimension.
For indicators with this aggregation method, the aggregated value for each reporting period will be computed by counting the number of sites for which the collected value exceed a threshold defined globally for the indicator. The aggregation between a group of reporting period will be performed with the usual methods of mean and sum, at the choice of the user, like for other indicators.
