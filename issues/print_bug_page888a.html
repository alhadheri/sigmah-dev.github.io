Summary0001101: Contact field required status not updated without refresh
DescriptionOn v2.2-rc6, if you set a value to a required contact field and click on "Save modifications" for this phase, the contact field won't be marked as filled ("green light") in the "Required fields" panel. Thus, the "Close this phase" button won't be enabled even if all other required fields are filled.

As soon as a required Contact field is filled, it should be marked as filled in the "Required fields" panel, and enable the "Close this phase" button if all other required fields are also filled.
Steps To Reproduce
Additional InformationThe current workaround for users is either to Refresh their screen (F5 shortcut), or to open another phase and come back to the phase where you had the issue.
