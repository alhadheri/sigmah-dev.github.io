Summary0001061: Field names not matching contact type in Contacts Global Export configuration
DescriptionOn v2.2-rc2, in the "Global Export of Contacts configuration" popup, you will see "First name", "Surname" and "Name of the organisation" fields for an organisation contact... and also for an individual.
And when you will produce the global export of contacts, you will get a "Family name" column in the file where you will have the name of organisation.

Fields and columns in configuration and generated global exports should match with contact types.
