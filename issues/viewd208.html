Summary0001043: No modification actually made after selecting changes made for duplicate
DescriptionOn v2.2-rc2, when creating a new contact which has potential duplicates from a list of contacts "create" button, if the user selects one of the possible duplicate and ask to modify one of its field, he will get the following result after clicking on the "Update contact" button:
- the requested modifications won't be applied in the selected duplicate
- a new tab will automatically open in the application on the selected duplicate which lead to losing the modification made on the project phase where the list of contacts field was

When requesting to edit a selected duplicate, selected modifications must be applied on the duplicate. And a new screen should not be opened if it leads to losing other modifications on a project phase.
