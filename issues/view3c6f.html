Summary0000193: Reminder email notification
DescriptionFor all "to do" reminders, a new option should give the possibility to send an email notification when creating the reminder.

This notification can have more than one recipient, and can have a comment. The email which will be fired will have:
- subject: the title of the reminder
- sender: author of the reminder
- body: contains the date of the reminder, project/orgunit to which the reminder is attached (code, title and an hyperlink to the project/orgunit on the title) and the optional comment.

When the reminder will be opened for modification, all the details fo the notification sent are accessible (expedition date, author, comment, recipient(s))

If the reminder is created through a "field-linked reminder incentive" (0000534), the name of the field and an hyperlink to this field are included in the notification email.
Additional InformationThere could be an alternative feature design to reply the same need to send email notifications.
In all notifications that a content has been modified, an hyperlink could be used to suggest to send a notification email with the detailled content of the modification.
In the configuration of a field, a new option "Ask to send a notification email in a popup for each modification" could be enabled which will open a popup each time a modification is made on the field, ask if the user wants or not to send a notification email.
