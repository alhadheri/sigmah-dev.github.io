Summary0001047: Error when adding a field in a contact model
DescriptionOn v2.2-rc2, there is always an Unexpected error each time the administrator adds any kind of field in a contact model.

This error has been reproduced on a fresh new contact model with the following field types: contact list, date, message.

After getting the Unexpected error, the field appears normally in the list of fields.
There is nothing in the logs tracing this error.
Additional InformationThis bug has been produced first by editing a contact model in maintenance mode. After that, the bug appears always.

But... much later on, the bug was no more reproduced on a newly created contact model, until reproduced on a contact model on which it was initially seen.
