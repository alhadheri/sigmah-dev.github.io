Summary0000411: Creation of a user/profile sometimes create two users/profiles
DescriptionA similar bug has been notified in the process of creation of users and profiles (see attached user report).
After completing the form, when the administrator clicks on the "Save" button, get gets no immediate feedack.. and the "Save" button remains enabled. Often, he clicks once again on the button because he doubts that something is under process.... and then he gets 2 users or 2 profiles instead of 1.

When creating a user/profile, a modal waiting message should pop up to inform the administrator that the creation is under way, and to prevent him from clicking twice on the "Save" button.
